Lasagna Recipe

Description: This easy white chicken lasagna with spinach is a different, yet delicious twist on a classic lasagna!

Chicken Lasagna is a delicacy that combines tender chicken, creamy cheese, and savory tomato sauce layered between sheets of pasta. This dish is a comforting and hearty meal that is perfect for family dinners or special occasions. The lasagna is typically made by layering cooked lasagna noodles with a mixture of seasoned ground chicken, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and marinara sauce. It is then baked until the cheese is melted and bubbly, creating a deliciously satisfying dish that is sure to please everyone at the table.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 3 cups heavy cream
  • 2 (10.5 ounce) cans condensed cream of mushroom soup

  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Mix heavy cream, cream of mushroom soup, Parmesan cheese, and butter in a saucepan over low heat; simmer, stirring frequently, until well blended.
  4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir onion in olive oil until tender, then add garlic and mushrooms.
  5. Mix in chicken, and cook until heated through. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Add 1/2 the chicken mixture, and 1 cup mozzarella. Top with 1/3 the cream sauce mixture, and repeat the layers.
    7. Place the remaining noodles on top, and spread with remaining sauce.
  7. Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, or until brown and bubbly. Top with the remaining mozzarella, and continue baking until cheese is melted and lightly browned.
